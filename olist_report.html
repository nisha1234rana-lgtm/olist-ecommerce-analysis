Data-Backed Findings

6 Strategic Insights

Key findings from SQL analysis, RFM segmentation, and machine learning models — with actionable business recommendations.

📈

Revenue Growth

Olist scaled 26× in 13 months — from 265 orders in Oct 2016 to 7,289 in Nov 2017. Black Friday 2017 peaked at R$987K in a single month, revealing strong seasonality dependency.

→ Build inventory and logistics capacity before Q4 every year to capture seasonal demand.

🚚

Delivery Drives Satisfaction

Early deliveries score 4.30★ vs 1.67★ for late orders — a 61% satisfaction collapse. 88,867 orders arrived early (avg 13.7 days ahead) while only 6,557 were late.

→ Maintain conservative delivery estimates. Arriving early is a measurable competitive advantage.

⚠️

Retention Crisis

35,888 customers (37% of base) are At Risk with R$4.99M revenue at stake. Every customer orders only once on average — repeat purchase rate is effectively 0%.

→ Launch email re-engagement campaigns targeting At Risk segment — highest ROI opportunity.

💳

Brazilian Payment Culture

73.9% pay by credit card with avg 3.5 installments — reflecting Brazil's parcelamento culture. Boleto accounts for 19%. Total payment value: R$16.01M across 103,886 transactions.

→ Offer more installment options and boleto promotions to increase purchase conversion rates.

📍

Geographic Concentration

São Paulo generates R$5.08M — 40% of all revenue with 40,501 orders. Top 3 states (SP, RJ, MG) drive 65% of revenue. Northern states show highest avg order values (R$175+).

→ Invest in logistics in northern states to unlock high-value untapped markets.

💄

Category Intelligence

Beauty & Health leads with R$1.23M revenue AND 4.19★ — rare combination of high revenue and satisfaction. Office Furniture scores lowest at 3.52★ despite R$268K revenue.

→ Prioritize Beauty & Health marketing. Urgently investigate Office Furniture quality issues.

How It Was Built

Methodology & Approach

A full end-to-end data pipeline from raw CSVs to machine learning models.

01

Data Collection & Storage

Loaded 7 real Olist CSV datasets into a MySQL 8.0 relational database. Designed normalized schema with foreign keys, indexes, and constraints. Total: 600,000+ rows across 7 tables.

02

SQL Analysis (15 Queries)

Wrote complex MySQL queries using CTEs, window functions (NTILE, LAG, RANK), subqueries, and multi-table JOINs. Covered revenue trends, cohort analysis, RFM scoring, delivery analysis, and state heatmaps.

03

Power BI Dashboard (5 Pages)

Built a 5-page interactive dashboard using Olist's official brand colors (#CE7B50, #3E84DF, #000818). Pages cover Executive Summary, Delivery & Payments, Customer RFM, Geographic Analysis, and Strategic Recommendations.

04

Machine Learning Models

Built 5 ML models in Python: RFM Segmentation, K-Means Clustering (K=4), Churn Prediction (Random Forest, AUC: 0.95+), CLV Forecasting (Gradient Boosting), and Revenue Trend Forecasting (Linear Regression).
